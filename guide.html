Fundraiser Guide

This guide explains how fundraising works on the Sisi Kwa Sisi platform and how individuals can either support a cause through donations or request assistance through a verified fundraising campaign.

Donating to a Fundraiser

Anyone can support verified fundraising campaigns listed on the platform. Every contribution helps individuals and families facing financial challenges such as medical needs, education support, or emergency assistance.

To donate:

  • Browse the list of active fundraisers on the platform.
  • Select the fundraiser you would like to support.
  • Click the Donate button.
  • Enter the amount you wish to contribute.
  • Complete the payment using M-Pesa.

After the payment is completed, your contribution will be recorded and added to the fundraiser's total amount raised.

Requesting Assistance

Sisi Kwa Sisi is committed to maintaining transparency and protecting donors by ensuring that every fundraiser is genuine and verified before it is published on the platform.

For this reason, fundraising campaigns are not created directly by users. Individuals who require financial assistance must first contact the Sisi Kwa Sisi team.

How to Request Help

  • Contact the Sisi Kwa Sisi team through the provided phone number or email.
  • Explain the situation and the type of support required.
  • Provide any relevant information or documentation related to the case.

The information provided will help the team assess the request and determine whether a fundraiser can be created.

Verification Process

Before any fundraiser is published, the Sisi Kwa Sisi team conducts a review process to confirm the authenticity of the request.

This process may include reviewing documentation, confirming the situation, and speaking with relevant individuals or institutions when necessary.

Verification helps ensure that donations reach genuine cases and maintains trust within the community.

Fundraiser Publication

If a request is verified and approved, a Sisi Kwa Sisi administrator will create and publish the fundraiser on the platform.

Once published, community members can view the fundraiser and make donations to support the cause.

Contact

If you need assistance or would like to request help, you can contact the Sisi Kwa Sisi team directly.
